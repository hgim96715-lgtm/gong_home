## 정답 쿼리 (Solution)

```sql
-- 여기에 작성한 쿼리를 붙여넣으세요.
SELECT r.category,r.sub_category,COUNT(DISTINCT order_id) as cnt_orders
FROM records r
JOIN customer_stats cs ON r.customer_id=cs.customer_id
WHERE r.order_date=cs.first_order_date
GROUP BY r.category,r.sub_category
ORDER BY cnt_orders DESC
```
